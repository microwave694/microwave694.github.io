Upon joining the game you will be greeted by an ominous green frame infront of you.

This is the field, and grass spawns here. To harvest the grass, walk up to it, simple as that.

There is a cap as to how much grass can be on the field at once, which you can see at the top of your screen.

Harvesting grass also grants XP, which will increment the level meter, once it fills up, the requirement will go up and you will get 1 perk

You will probably want to get Upgrades, which you can purchase right infront of the field, by simply clicking on the upgrade you want and then pressing buy. Its that simple.

You will unlock stuff by getting more levels, of which the first thing you unlock is at Level 5.

When reaching level 5 you unlock a new building to the left of the field.

The automation building will allow you to purchase crucial automation early on in the game.

Currently there only is one upgrade, which is the autocut upgrade. The autocut upgrade simply cuts grass for you. More upgrades will unlock later.

One other thing to note before your first prestige is that every 10 levels have a 30 second timer in which you have to get the xp requirement, failure to do so will reset the xp requirement and timer, thus limiting your max level.
